ABOUT US

WELCOME TO ROBOTICS CLUB!!".

Our club mainly works with student-body relationships under the Technical Club of the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Bhagalpur. In the last few years robotics activities in India has moved well beyond the traditional areas of industrial applications, atomic energy, etc. and entered newer domains of education, rehabilitation, entertainment, and even into our homes. Our Club consists of over enthusiast members with high interest in new technologies and believes in the practice of overall development through working on a hands-on project and live events like Hackathons.
