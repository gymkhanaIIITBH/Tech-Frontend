ABOUT US

WELCOME TO ROBOTICS CLUB!!".

Our club mainly works with student-body relationships under the Technical Club of the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Bhagalpur. In the last few years robotics activities in India has moved well beyond the traditional areas of industrial applications, atomic energy, etc. and entered newer domains of education, rehabilitation, entertainment, and even into our homes. Our Club consists of over enthusiast members with high interest in new technologies and believes in the practice of overall development through working on a hands-on project and live events like Hackathons.

P R O J E C T S

HOME AUTOMATION USING ARDINO

In this we Have multiple sensors like PIR sensor, Ping sensor, Gas sensor,Temperature sensor which all together control the lightning,exhaust control.etc

AUTOMATIC DOOR LOCK

In, this project, I have built an aurdino Keypad Door Lock which can be mounted to any of your existing doors to secure them with digital password.

GARAGE AUTOMATION

Ultrasonic sensors have been used to detect the arrival or departure of a vehicle towards the garage and shutter opens and closes accordingly.

BURGLAR ALARM

Works on the use of PIR sensor. Incase of any unusual movement PIR sensor detects it and signal it through an alarm, red light and LCD screen.
