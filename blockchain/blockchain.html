About Us

Blockchain is a decentralised concept and it doesn't need intermediaries during transactions thus making it world's leading software platform for digital assets. It is a distributed database that is shared among the nodes of a computer network. This technology can be most simply defined distributed ledger that records the provenance of a digital asset. The innovation with a blockchain is that it guarantees the fidelity and security of a record of data and generates trust without the need for a trusted third party. The overall objective is to facilitate learning, exploration, and discussion on blockchain technology and to explore avenues of facilitation such as business, mining and finance.
