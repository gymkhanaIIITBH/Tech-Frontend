ELECTROVOLT

Electronics Club Of IIIT Bhagalpur

Who are we and Why ?

We are part of Tech Board IIIT Bhagalpur as Electronics Club

The Electronics club was created by electronic hobbyists that strive hard to impart enthusiasm and passion for electronics and allied fields among students. It envisions nurturing the engineering maturity by giving practical hands on exposure to various domains. In this era of rapidly evolving technologies we strive to make them aware of the various technological advancements happening around the globe. We believe in imagination, Innovation and Application. We organize various workshops, lectures, competitions and projects throughout the year to bring out the “TECHIE” in you We aim at providing students with the basic knowledge which will help them to come up with something creative and constructive which is useful for society. We motivate students to develop skills, solve complex problems and come up with new innovative ideas with proper guidelines like skillsets required and bounded time duration. This not only leads to professionalism but also plays a major role in developing hard as well as soft skills. The emphasis is always laid on experimenting and learning with no prerequisites required

What is Electronics?

The Science that deals with the behavior and control of electrons in vacuums and gases, and with the use of electron tubes, photoelectric cells, transistors is called electronics.

Electronics is the branch of physics and technology that deals with circuits, transistors, microchips, and the behaviour and movement of electrons. It handles electric circuits containing active and passive elemens and uses underlying techniques. It is an important part of engineering. Electronic devices have become an important part of our day-to-day life. It has become difficult for us to do work without using electronic devices.
