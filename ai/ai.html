AI-ML Club of IIIT Bhagalpur is for all futuristic ones.This club promises to make system adaptive through artificial intelligence.We organize webinars, debates, and various competition along with hackathons to show up your skills and improve the abilities to analyze, and develop computational intelligence. Our main goal is to administer you with the industrial challenges for your better future. This club gives everyone equal opprtunity to show up skills of analyzing problems.
